# F965 — Scratchpad

## Purpose
Captures §965 transition tax data from Forms 965-A through 965-E for individual taxpayers.
The §965 transition tax (TCJA 2017) required US shareholders of certain foreign corporations
to include previously untaxed foreign earnings in income as of tax year 2017.
