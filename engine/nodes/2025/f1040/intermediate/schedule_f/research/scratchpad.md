# Schedule F Scratchpad

## Key Findings

### Drake Screen
- Screen code: F
- filed_tax_node_type_code: "schedule_f"

### IRS Instructions Key Points (TY2025)
- Standard mileage rate: 70 cents/mile (2025)
- Section 179 max: $2,500,000
- Net profit (line 34) → Schedule 1 line 6 AND Schedule SE line 1a
- Two accounting methods: cash (Parts I+II) and accrual (Parts I+II+III)

### Part I — Farm Income (Cash Method, lines 1–8)
- Line 1: Sales of livestock and other resale items
- Line 2: Cost or other basis of livestock/items on line 1
- Line 3a: Cooperative distributions (total)
- Line 3b: Cooperative distributions (taxable amount)
- Line 4a: Agricultural program payments (total)
- Line 4b: Agricultural program payments (taxable)
- Line 5a: CCC loans reported under election
- Line 5b: CCC loans forfeited
- Line 5c: CCC loans forfeited (elected earlier)
- Line 6a: Crop insurance proceeds received 2025
- Line 6b: Crop insurance (taxable in 2025)
- Line 6d: Crop insurance deferred from 2024
- Line 7: Custom hire/machine work income
- Line 8: Other farm income
- Line 9: Gross farm income (sum of lines 1–8 plus adjustments)

### Part II — Farm Expenses (Cash Method, lines 10–32f)
- Line 10: Car and truck expenses (Form 4562 or standard mileage)
- Line 11: Chemicals
- Line 12: Conservation expenses (max 25% of gross income from farming)
- Line 13: Custom hire/machine work
- Line 14: Depreciation/section 179 (Form 4562)
- Line 15: Employee benefit programs
- Line 16: Feed purchased
- Line 17: Fertilizers and lime
- Line 18: Freight and trucking
- Line 19: Gasoline, fuel, and oil
- Line 20: Insurance (other than health)
- Line 21a: Mortgage interest (paid to banks/etc.)
- Line 21b: Other interest
- Line 22: Labor hired (net FICA)
- Line 23: Pension/profit-sharing plans
- Line 24a: Rent/lease of vehicles, machinery, equipment
- Line 24b: Rent/lease of other property (land, pasture)
- Line 25: Repairs and maintenance
- Line 26: Seeds and plants
- Line 27: Storage and warehousing
- Line 28: Supplies
- Line 29: Taxes
- Line 30: Utilities
- Line 31: Veterinary, breeding, medicine
- Line 32a–32f: Other expenses
- Line 32f: Total other expenses
- Line 33: Total expenses before line 34
- Line 34: Net profit or (loss)

### Part III — Farm Income (Accrual Method, lines 36–43)
- Lines 38a–40c mirror Part I but for accrual

### Line 34 Routing
- → Schedule 1, line 6 (all individuals)
- → Schedule SE, line 1a (net farm profit for SE tax)
- → Form 8995/8995-A (QBI deduction if qualified)
- → Form 8582 (if passive — material participation = No)
- → Form 6198 (if at-risk box 36b checked)
- → Form 461 (excess business loss limitation)

### Special Rules
- Conservation expenses: 25% of gross farm income limit
- At-risk rules apply (box 36a/36b)
- Passive activity rules apply (line E material participation)
- EBL applies (Form 461) — same thresholds as schedule_c
